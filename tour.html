All the Features you Could Need

With On The Run task manager there are many features available to make your life easier, organized, and stress free.

Notifications

No more worry about keeping track of your due dates. Your schedule is organized in a simple calendar that sends you notifcations when a deadline is approaching.

Online Storage

The online cloud is an easy place to store your documents and access them anywhere, anytime online.

Data Transfer

Sharing files online hasn’t been easier. No limits to the size of the documents or the amount of information that needs to be transfered.

Media Formats

You will be able to access this application on many media formats anywhere, anytime from whatever device you are using.

Group Sharing

Your are not only able to share files online with other designers but you are able to work on a project at the same time. The finished product is in one place accessible to all colborators to share with the client.

Project Boards

With the project boards you will be able to manage all your projects in one place. You can work on them online and with the autosave feature you don’t need to worry about your computer crashing or needing to back it up.
